Welcome to the Feedback Application

Overview:

Our Feedback Application is designed to collect and display user feedback in a simple and intuitive way. Whether you have suggestions, comments, or just want to share your thoughts, we want to hear from you!

Features:

  • Submit Feedback: Use our easy-to-fill feedback form to share your experiences or suggestions.

  • View Feedback: Check out what others have said by visiting the feedback page.

  • Summary: Get a quick overview of all submissions, including the total number of responses and the latest feedback.

How It Works:

  • Navigate to the Feedback form: Click the link below to go the feedback form page.

  • Submit your feedback: Fill out your name and comments, then submit your form.

  • View Submitted Feedback: Go to the feedback page to see all the feedback submitted by users.

  • Check the summary: Visit the summary page for an overview of all submissions.
